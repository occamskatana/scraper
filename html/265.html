About This Organization:

Bayshore Retreat is an exclusive treatment center that specializes in truly individualized drug and alcohol programs. Our six (6) clients-at-a-time format means the addiction, its causes, and the possible damage are addressed intimately and personally. The home environment and serene water front location in beautiful Destin, Florida offer an atmosphere for healing from the very beginning. Our holistic approach, along with traditional therapy techniques, provides an integrated program that treats the physical, spiritual, and psychological aspects of addiction with a combination of diet, exercise, dry-sauna therapy, deep tissue massages, yoga, and off site real life activities. In addition, clients receive approximately 30 hours a week of quality counseling, which include individual, group, life skills and family.
